About Us

E-Cell IIT Hyderabad aims to create a platform to promote entrepreneurial spirit and discuss ideas among students and budding start-ups via different events and competitions. E-Cell IIT Hyderabad brings Campus Ambassador Programme to create a network amongst various colleges across Hyderabad on a larger scale. We invite you to join this initiative and work along with us to make an idea into an innovation.

Why CA

An opportunity to expand your network in your campus and outside.

Opportunity to be part of various entrepreneurial events conducted by E-Cell IIT Hyderabad.

Improve your management and communication skills by being a part of CA IIT Hyderabad.

Get access to resources to enhance your knowledge about entrepreneurship and Management.

Terms & Conditions

All the events held under this programme would be Supervised by E-Cell IIT Hyderabad directly.

In case of any dispute, the final decision lies with E-Cell IITH.
